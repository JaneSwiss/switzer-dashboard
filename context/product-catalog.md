## Product 1: 3-in-1 Business Bundle — $82 USD (flagship)

**What's included:**
- A premade Wix website (ready to customise and publish)
- A full branding kit (1,000+ Canva templates covering social media,
  logos, business cards, flyers, ebooks, and more)
- A Canva landing page

Everything is designed in one cohesive visual style so the brand,
website, and landing page all look like they belong together.

**Who it's for:**
Coaches, consultants, service providers, and female small business owners
who are starting out or rebranding and want a complete, professional
online presence without piecing it together from multiple sources.

**The core problem it solves:**
Most business owners either spend months trying to figure out design
and tech themselves, or they pay thousands to hire someone. This bundle
gives them a professional result in days, not months.

**Key selling angles:**
- Everything in one place - brand, website, and landing page
- Cohesive look across all platforms from day one
- No design skills needed - just customise the text and photos
- No need to hire a designer or a web developer separately
- Works for Wix (no extra platform subscription needed beyond Wix's own)

**Upsell logic:**
Best positioned as the primary offer for new customers who don't yet
have a website or branding kit. Do NOT pitch this to existing buyers
of Wix websites or branding kits - they already own the core components.

**Objections to address:**
- "I don't know how to use Wix" - Wix is one of the easiest website
  builders available, no coding required
- "Will it look generic?" - All designs are modern, editorial, and
  premium - hard to tell they're templates
- "Is this for my niche?" - Works for any service-based business as
  long as the customer likes the aesthetic

**How to talk about it:**
Lead with the outcome (professional online presence fast), then explain
what's included. Never list features without connecting each one to a
business benefit.

**Example copy angle:**
"Instead of spending months figuring out your brand, your website, and
your landing page separately - this bundle gives you all three, designed
to match, for less than the cost of one hour with a freelance designer."

## General product copy rules

**Always:**
- Lead with the outcome, then explain what's included
- Connect each feature to a specific business benefit
- Use the proof points naturally (27,700+ sales, top 1% Etsy seller)
- Make it easy to understand what the customer is getting and why it matters

**Never:**
- List features without connecting them to a benefit
- Use phrases like "stunning", "gorgeous", or "amazing"
- Oversell or use hype language
- Imply that a product will magically fix a business without effort

**Pricing context (for email and blog copy):**
The 3-in-1 bundle at $82 is less than the cost of one hour with a
freelance designer. The branding kit at $38 replaces weeks of DIY
design work. These comparisons make the price feel obvious, not cheap.
